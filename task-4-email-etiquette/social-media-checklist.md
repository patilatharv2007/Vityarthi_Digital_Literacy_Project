## Social Media Do’s
● Maintain a clean and professional online profile

● Think twice before posting any content online

● Communicate in a respectful and positive manner

● Keep your personal and sensitive information secure

● Use strong, unique passwords for better account safety

● Verify information before sharing it with others

● Interact politely and respectfully with all users

● Always acknowledge and credit original creators

● Regularly check and update your privacy settings

● Share meaningful, informative, and quality content

● Be aware of your audience and digital footprint

● Use social media as a platform for learning and growth

● Report any inappropriate or suspicious activity

● Balance your time between online and real-life activities

● Follow the rules and guidelines of each platform

## Social Media Don’ts
● Do not share personal or confidential information publicly

● Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or harmful content

● Never spread misinformation or fake news

● Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments

● Avoid oversharing details about your personal life

● Do not use weak or easily guessable passwords

● Never copy or share content without permission

● Do not neglect privacy and security settings

● Avoid posting content in anger or emotional situations

● Do not open or trust unknown links and messages

● Never participate in bullying, trolling, or harassment

● Avoid excessive use of social media platforms
