TASK 5

I selected ransomware as the topic for my case study because it is one of the most serious and rapidly growing cyber threats.
Ransomware is a type of malicious software that blocks access to a system or encrypts important data, and then demands payment (ransom) from the victim to restore access. For this task, I prepared a detailed case study explaining how ransomware attacks typically occur, such as through phishing emails, malicious downloads, or unsafe websites. I also described the common targets, including students, individuals, and organizations, and the consequences like data loss, financial damage, and privacy risks.
